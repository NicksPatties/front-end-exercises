Notes

CSS variables cannot be animated!

I didn't know this until after I calculated all of the elements based on the position of the sun. The goal was to animate the sun, and then have everything else (the pyramid shadown position, the pyramid color, the sky color) be based on the sun's location.

When I attempted to animate the X position of the sun, the sun jumped from one position to the next, instead of easing from one location to another. To fix this, I can do one of two things:

  • Assign custom CSS properties, which can be animated, but has limited browser support.
  • Move calculations into existing properties.

For better support, I'll move the calculations into their own properties. Animate properties, NOT variables.
